## 2. THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

### The "Tab-Switching Tax"
The average content team uses **5+ tools** to run their social presence:
- **Buffer / Later** to schedule
- **Copy.ai / ChatGPT** to draft
- **Canva** to design
- **Notion / Google Docs** to plan
- **Sheets / Airtable** to track performance

These tools don't talk to each other. The AI doesn't know what performed well last week. Canva doesn't know your brand colors. Buffer can't tell the AI that Thursday 7:30 AM is your best posting time. The team wastes 70-80% of their time on logistics, not strategy.

### Three Core Pains We Eliminate

**Pain 1: Tab-Switching Tax**
Five tools, zero shared context. Content teams spend hours copying text between tools, reformatting for different platforms, and manually tracking what worked.

**Pain 2: Generic AI Output**
Templated openers. Buzzword soup. Posts that sound like everyone else, because the model has never read your brand guidelines, your past posts, or your audience data.

**Pain 3: No Learning Loop**
You publish. A post goes viral. The tool forgets. Same generic suggestions next week, regardless of what crushed it or flopped.

**Daky's Answer:** One platform that holds all your brand context, generates posts that sound like *you*, schedules them at the right times, and feeds performance data back into the AI to sharpen every future draft.

## 3. PLATFORM FEATURES (DETAILED)

### 3.1 Nova AI — Brand-Aware Content Generation

The core AI engine. Nova learns your brand from the ground up and generates content that passes the "did a human write this?" test.

**What Nova knows about your brand:**
- Brand voice, tone, and personality
- Product descriptions and key messages
- Target audience and demographics
- Past content performance (what resonated, what flopped)
- Platform-specific formatting preferences
- Competitor positioning

**What Nova generates:**
- Full social posts (hook, body, CTA, hashtags)
- Thread structures for X (Twitter)
- Long-form LinkedIn articles
- Caption variations for Instagram
- TikTok video scripts
- Facebook event/community posts

**Platform-specific intelligence:**
Nova reformats automatically for each channel. A LinkedIn thought leadership post is different from an Instagram carousel caption — Nova knows the difference and applies platform-specific length, tone, hashtag count, and CTA style without you having to ask.

**Step-by-step workflow (visible to user in real time):**
1. Search existing brand designs for style context
2. Extract style prompts and brand signals
3. Tailor content to brand voice and audience
4. Generate the image or draft
5. Save to design history

A dedicated creative generation engine inside the platform. Users describe what they want; the AI produces professional visuals in seconds.

**Generation Modes:**

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Image | Generate static social media visuals, posters, covers, ad creatives |
| Video | Generate short-form video clips for Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts |

**Image AI Models:**

|-------|---------|-------------|----------|

**Video AI Models:**

| Model | Quality | Credit Cost | Best For |
|-------|---------|-------------|----------|

**AI Studio workflow:**
1. User describes what they need ("dark green agency poster with neon tape bands")
2. Nova searches the Discover library for inspiration and style context
3. Nova extracts style prompts, colors, and composition guidance
4. Selected AI model generates the image or video
5. Asset saved to user's History tab with full metadata

**Context-aware suggestions:** Nova surfaces personalized generation prompts based on the user's past creations and brand memory, so there's always a starting point.

### 3.3 Canvas Design Builder

A professional, browser-based design tool powered by Fabric.js — think Canva-level editing, but integrated directly into the content workflow.

**What users can build:**
- Social media cards (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok covers)
- Branded post templates
- Story and carousel slides
- Ad creatives
- Any custom canvas size

**Design capabilities:**
- Text layers: rich formatting, custom fonts, colors, opacity
- Shapes: rectangles, ellipses, lines — fully customizable
- Image upload: paste from clipboard or upload from device
- Layer management: bring to front/back, group, lock
- Precise controls: position, size, rotation, opacity, stroke, fill

**Canvas presets (one click):**
- Instagram Post (1080×1080), Story (1080×1920)
- Facebook Post (1200×630)
- Twitter/X Post (1200×675)
- LinkedIn Post (1200×627)
- TikTok Cover (1080×1920)
- Custom (any dimension)

**Productivity features:**
- Undo/Redo (up to 60 steps) — Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y
- Duplicate: Ctrl+D
- Save: Ctrl+S
- Keyboard arrow nudge for precision placement
- Export to PNG or JPG at any resolution

**Admin-curated templates:** Admins can publish professional design templates to the Discover library. Users browse in masonry layout and click to use any template as a starting point. Currently seeded with professional templates including the UpDraft Agency Tape poster and arcgraphix Editorial Carousel Cover.

**Available actions:**
- Generate and schedule posts across selected channels

**Example workflows:**

- "Every Monday at 8 AM → generate a week of Instagram captions based on this month's campaign brief"
- "When form submission arrives → create a segmented welcome email sequence"

### 3.6 Analytics Dashboard

A unified analytics view across all connected platforms that closes the learning loop by feeding performance data back into the AI.

**What's tracked:**
- Reach, impressions, engagement rate per post
- Follower growth over time
- Best performing post breakdown

**The learning loop:**
This is Daky's most important differentiator. Every metric collected feeds back into Nova's context. Posts that perform above average become positive training signals. Posts that underperform are flagged. Nova uses this data to generate better content every week automatically — without the user doing anything extra.

### Key Competitive Advantages

**vs. Buffer:** Buffer is a scheduling tool. It does not generate content, design visuals, or learn your brand. Dakyworld Hub replaces Buffer and adds an entire AI creative suite on top.

**vs. Hootsuite:** Hootsuite is expensive ($99+/month) and built for enterprise scheduling management, not AI creative generation. It has no canvas builder, no image/video AI, and no learning loop. Dakyworld Hub does all of this for a fraction of the price.

**vs. Canva Pro:** Canva is a design tool. It cannot generate captions, schedule posts, or learn from analytics. Dakyworld Hub includes a Canva-level canvas builder AND everything Canva cannot do.

**vs. Copy.ai / Jasper:** These are generic writing tools. They don't know your brand. They don't schedule. They don't design. They can't generate images or videos. Dakyworld Hub generates content that sounds like *you*, then takes it all the way from draft to published.

**vs. Sprout Social:** Sprout Social is enterprise-priced ($199+/month) and focused on listening and analytics, not creative generation. It has no design builder and no AI image/video generation. Dakyworld Hub is the more complete, more affordable alternative.

### The "Daky Moat" — Why Competitors Can't Easily Copy This

1. **Brand memory compounds over time.** The longer a brand uses Daky, the better the AI gets at generating content that sounds like them. This creates a switching cost that deepens every week.

2. **Full-stack creative + distribution.** No competitor currently combines professional design building, multi-model AI image/video generation, social scheduling, automation, and analytics in one product at this price point.

3. **Credit model democratizes AI access.** Instead of paying per seat (which penalizes larger teams) or paying unlimited (which prices out small creators), Daky's credit model lets any team size find the right plan for their actual usage.
